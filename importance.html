Why is WebRTC Important?

WebRTC simplifies the development of real-time communication applications by providing APIs that handle complex tasks like signaling, encryption, and media streaming.

It eliminates the need for third-party plugins, enhancing security and performance while reducing latency for users.

With its ability to work across browsers and devices, WebRTC has revolutionized industries like telehealth, online education, and remote work.
